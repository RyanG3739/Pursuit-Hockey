U14

Welcome to our U14 camp where we take the biggest step in getting each player ready for the high school level. This skate will work on the players power, escape skills, puckhandling at speed, and footwork. Entering highschool involves meeting a higher competition, this camp is for players looking to be pushed to make the jump into a higher pace of play and competition. Players should expect to know how to give and take a hit at this camp to excel their game and experience.
